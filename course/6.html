GLOBAL HOME - Master Class

Description

Using the film 'Global Home' this master class treats the chances and challenges of an episode film as a cinematic form.

About the film

As soon as filmmaker Eva Stotz found out about the Couchsurfing network, she had to know more. What was this online community providing a free place to sleep anywhere in the world, a chance look in on the lives of complete strangers, and an opportunity to reach out beyond the internet after only a few emails? Eva packed her camera and set off on her travels.

Course content

  • Development of a film from a personal journey to universal meaning
  • Chances and challenges of an episode film as a cinematic form
  • On filmmaking in a foreign country
  • Being the director and cameraperson at the same time
  • Exchange on topics treated by the film like sharing economy and the personal experience of globalization
