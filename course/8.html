Two week workshop

Labour in a single shot

Description

'Labour in a Single Shot' is a project that Antje Ehmann and Harun Farocki started in 2011. They have initiated video production workshops in 15 cities worldwide followed by a series of exhibitions that shows selected workshop results in a larger context. Since 2017 Eva Stotz continues those workshops, starting in Juin 2017 in Vilnius, Lithuania. Continuing together with Antje Ehmann in March 2018 in Marseille, France, September 2019 in Chicago, USA, 2020 in Bogota, Columbia and Shanghai, China. The task of the workshops is to produce videos of 1 to 2 minutes in length, each taken in a single shot. The camera can be static, panning or travelling – only cuts are not allowed. The subject of investigation is ‘labour’. More here: http://www.labour-in-a-single-shot.net

Course content

Some content that comes up is:

  • How to film labour?
  • How to compose a narration without editing?
  • How to use sound as a narrative element?
